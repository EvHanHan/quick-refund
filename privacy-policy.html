Privacy Policy for 1-click navan refund

Last updated: March 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the 1-click navan refund Chrome extension processes information when you use it.

What this extension does

The extension helps you retrieve telecom/transit invoices from supported provider websites and prefill a Navan expense upload flow.

Information we process

  • Website content and page metadata: read from supported provider websites and Navan pages to find invoice elements and upload form fields.
  • Invoice/receipt content: invoice files (such as PDFs) required to complete the upload flow.
  • Local settings and state: selected provider, billing type, reminder preferences, and temporary workflow state saved in chrome.storage.local.

How information is used

  • To provide the core extension workflow (invoice retrieval and form prefilling).
  • To support user-enabled reminder notifications.
  • To keep temporary state while a flow is in progress.

Data sharing and selling

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • We do not use your data for credit or lending purposes.

Remote services and network access

The extension connects only to domains required for the feature flow (supported provider websites, Navan, and related login pages). It may also check version metadata from raw.githubusercontent.com.

The extension does not download or execute remote code. Executable logic is packaged inside the extension itself.

Storage and retention

Data is stored locally in your browser. Temporary flow state may be cleaned automatically. You can clear extension data at any time via Chrome extension settings.

Security

Access is limited to the permissions and host domains declared in the extension manifest and used only to support user-initiated functionality.

Children's privacy

This extension is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the "Last updated" date on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the extension publisher via the support information listed in the Chrome Web Store.
